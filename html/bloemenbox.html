Bloemenbox

I created an app meant to connect the user with people both offline and online. Called the flower box. The flower box is accessible to people with visual impairments.

Mood music while scrolling

The flower box consists of 3 parts. The app, the game and the box.

Visually impaired

users: The app is entirely out of voice command. The user uses only his own voice, click and double click. On the box are Braille and there is a tray inside with dried flowers to feel and smell. The game consists of sounds and clicking.

With the phone you can search for the different boxes scattered in a park. Via the maps you can see where the different boxes are placed. When you click on a box it will show you the way. Through the app you can find another person who also has the app. You can then send a request to play with them. When the other person has accepted your request, he/she will also be shown the way to go. So when two people are at the box you can scan the QR code on the box. This way the app keeps track of which boxes you have already had. When the game is played the users will get information about the flower and get flower seeds with it. so the user can collect all different flower seeds. The boxes consist of solar energy.

3D Model

To better visualize my design, I created a 3D model in Blendr. This is the flower box that will be in the park. Here the user can play the nature game with his buddy. After the game the user will get information about the flower and will get seeds from that flower. For people with visual impairment, this box is completely accessible. On the box are Braille and it is a sounds game.

The box also has a drawer, in this drawer will be dried flowers that the user can feel and smell to get a better experience.
